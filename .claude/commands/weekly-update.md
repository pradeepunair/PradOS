---
name: weekly-update
description: Draft a weekly stakeholder update email from the week's work
---

Read Tasks/active.md, recent entries in Tasks/archive/, and any Projects/ briefs with recent activity.

Draft a stakeholder update in this format:

**Subject:** Weekly Update — Week of [date]

**Highlights this week:**
- [2-3 significant accomplishments]

**In progress:**
- [Key active work with expected completion]

**Coming up next week:**
- [1-2 priorities]

**Risks or needs:**
- [Anything requiring stakeholder attention or decisions. If none, omit this section.]

Tone: professional, concise, confident. No filler phrases.
Length: under 200 words for the body.

After drafting, ask: "Who should this go to? I can save a copy to _temp/ or a specific project folder."
