02 Task Lifecycle every task follows the same path
0
Capture
_temp/ or backlog
Idea hits. Dump it immediately. Zero friction — entropy is fine here.
1
Backlog
Tasks/backlog.md
Triage weekly. Assign rough priority. Not a commitment — just inventory.
2
Active
Tasks/active.md
Committed this week. Max 5 items. Blocked items tracked with reason.
3
Execute
Projects/ or Workflows/
Real work happens in Project folders or Workflow mini-workspaces.
4
Archive
Tasks/archive/
Completed with date stamp. Learnings promoted to Knowledge/.

06 Core Principles the philosophy behind PradOS
1
Pointers over content
CLAUDE.md doesn't contain everything — it points to where things live. Files stay lean. Depth lives at the leaves.
2
Capture before organizing
_temp/ exists because friction kills capture. Dump first. Entropy is fine in _temp. It is not acceptable anywhere else.
3
Every task has a lifecycle
Ideas are born in backlog. They grow in active. They graduate to archive. Nothing stays in limbo indefinitely.
4
Workflows multiply leverage
A process written once can run a hundred times with Claude. The Workflows/ factory compounds your effort over time.
5
Intelligence compounds through writing
A meeting note today is Knowledge/ next year. Output captured is context preserved. The system works when you write things down.
